Competitions

The Delftsche Dodgers will be participating in its first ever competition season in the 2023-24 year run by Dodgeball Bond Nederland (DBN). Instead of weekly matchups between clubs, there will be six competition days spread out throughout the season (roughly once every 1-2 months) in which all teams converge at one club and play each other. Participation in competitions as part of the Delftsche Dodgers requires a full year or half year competition membership. Not interested in competitions? You can also sign up as a basic member as well! Learn more about the Dutch national dodgeball league at DBN's website: https://dodgeballbondnederland.nl/
